What does OpenAI SMB Channel Partner status actually mean?

OpenAI SMB Channel Partner status means AI Build Group is authorised to sell ChatGPT Business with partner pricing to UK and Ireland organisations, alongside setup and rollout support. It does not mean AI Build Group is OpenAI, or that OpenAI directly delivers the consulting and governance work — that comes from the AI Build Group team.

In practice, the status is a distribution and support relationship: OpenAI sets and controls the ChatGPT Business product and licence terms; AI Build Group's role is to make the licence accessible with UK-based support wrapped around it. Nothing about partner status changes what ChatGPT Business does as a product — central billing, admin controls, and business data handling terms are identical whether you buy through a partner or direct.

It is worth being precise about what "channel partner" means in this context, because the term gets used loosely. It is not a reseller relationship where a third party repackages or rebrands the product — ChatGPT Business remains OpenAI's product, sold under OpenAI's terms, with the same licence whoever you buy it from. The channel partner layer is specifically about UK market access and support: AI Build Group can issue discount codes to eligible organisations and provide setup, training, and governance support that OpenAI's own sales motion does not typically include for smaller UK teams.
