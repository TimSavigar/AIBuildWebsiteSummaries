## Security and compliance

**Is ChatGPT Business secure for company data?** Business workspaces provide organisation ownership, admin controls, and business data handling terms distinct from consumer accounts. Map your own data flows and retention needs as part of rollout.

**Does Business reduce shadow AI?** Yes — when paired with useful workflows and clear policies. Give teams a governed workspace better than personal accounts, not just a ban.

## Governance and admin

**What admin controls are included?** Member management, central billing, usage visibility, data handling settings, and approved integration patterns. Confirm SSO needs early if required.

**Should we ban personal ChatGPT?** Bans alone fail. Pair policy with a sanctioned workspace and training so staff choose the approved path instead of going further underground.

## Rollout and support

**How long does rollout take?** Two to six weeks for many UK SMEs with a pilot cohort, three workflows, and basic governance.

**Why buy through AI Build Group?** UK OpenAI SMB Channel Partner — partner pricing, setup support, governance playbooks, and measured adoption help beyond a licence transaction.

## Comparing plans

**Is ChatGPT Business different from ChatGPT Plus?** Yes — Business adds organisation ownership, admin controls, central billing, and business data protections. Plus is for individuals, not governed team rollout.

**When should we consider ChatGPT Enterprise instead of Business?** Only when you need advanced SSO, very large scale, or enterprise-grade commercial controls — most UK SMBs start with Business for speed and can upgrade later.
